The three teachings

Seek. Study. Preserve.

Seek

The first teaching is attention. Look closer. Look lower. The hidden thing is often the living thing.

Study

Preserve

Rooted in Mystery. Grown to Inspire.

World Champignons began with a whisper from the forest floor.

Not the kind of whisper you hear with your ears. The older kind. The kind that arrives through the soles of your feet, through damp soil, through the strange electricity of standing among trees and realizing the world is not a machine.

The mushroom was the first teacher. Before maps pretended the world was finished, the mushroom appeared after rain and offered humanity a question: what else is hidden beneath the surface?
